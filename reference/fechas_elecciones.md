# Election dates

This dataset contains the dates of the local, general and european
elections in Spain from 1977 to 2023.

## Usage

``` r
fechas_elecciones
```

## Format

A dataset with the dates of the elections

- tipo_eleccion:

  Election level (local, general or european parliament)

- anno:

  Year of the election

- mes:

  Month of the election

- day:

  Day of the election

## Source

<https://www.juntaelectoralcentral.es/cs/jec/elecciones>
